====================
read-GRACE-geocenter
====================

Reads geocenter data from
`Sutterley et al. (2019) <https://doi.org/10.3390/rs11182108>`_
for updating Level-2 spherical harmonic data from the
NASA/DLR Gravity Recovery and Climate Experiment (GRACE) and the
NASA/GFZ Gravity Recovery and Climate Experiment Follow-On (GRACE-FO) missions
